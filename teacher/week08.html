Instructor Preparation

Overview

The team activity for this week has the students use a third-party API. They will be pulling data from swapi.co. They will need to remember how to deal with the promises that Fetch will return. The solution is given with promises and with async/await.

Also, encourage the students to record their questions while reading and give them to you to be addressed.

Meeting Time

The reading for this week introduces CSS transitions and animations. An example I often use is to start with these boxes and will change them (transform, size shape color, location) with transitions to demo the basics. You can finish with a bouncing ball or make a box go around the content div (slide corner to corner all the way around). Then you might build something like this: PokeApi Demo to review Ajax and add the sliding transition effect.
